PORTER PACKAGING

At Porter Packaging we design, manufacture and distribute packaging for brands. From the initial concept through to the mass production, our experienced team will deliver packaging that is on-brand, on-budget and on-time.

We provide genuine innovation in the development of your packaging with our unique blend of structures, materials, finishes, and print techniques as well as our understanding of fashion and trends. And we guarantee that we will never be beaten on price based on a like-for-like specification.

CUSTOM AND WHOLESALE
PACKAGING SOLUTIONS

We have the ability to supply your business with wholesale and/or custom packaging orders, whether it is for a large operation or a small project. Our knowledgeable team can help streamline the process and work with you to keep costs down while ensuring high quality results.

ECO AND RECYCLABLE PACKAGING

While all of our packaging is already Eco friendly and Recyclable we have a range of options available to those who wish to have packaging made from post consumer recycled products. These products are made from 100% post-consumer recycled paper pulp and are completely sustainable.
